# AutoCase: AI-Powered Legal Research 

AutoCase is an intelligent legal assistant that leverages **multi-agent AI collaboration** to automate legal research, citation management, summarization, and legal document drafting. It is designed to reduce manual effort, improve accuracy, and significantly speed up legal workflows for lawyers, law students, and legal researchers.

### Agent Responsibilities

- **Retriever Agent:** Searches legal databases and precedents  
- **Relevance Agent:** Filters and ranks retrieved documents  
- **Summarizer Agent:** Extracts key points and citations  
- **Writer Agent:** Drafts structured legal documents  
- **Critique Agent:** Reviews accuracy, logic, and formatting  
- **Final Draft:** Produces a polished, citation-ready output  

Each agent focuses on a distinct subtask, ensuring **modular, scalable, and reliable AI outputs**.
